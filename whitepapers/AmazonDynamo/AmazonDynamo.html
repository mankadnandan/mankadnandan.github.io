Assimilation of Dynamo: Amazons Highly Available Key-value Store ============== Link to the white paper: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1812.07527 ## Abstract - Reliability is very important at massive scale at which Amazon operates. Even a slightest outage causes significant financial consequences and impacts customer trust. - Amazon.com, the e-commerce site, runs on top of an infrastructure which consists of tens of thousands of servers and network components, spread across many data centers across the world. - At this scale of operations, it is to be assumed that small and large components continuously fail. - The way the persistent state is managed in face of these failures, drives the scalability and the reliability of the software systems. - This paper presents Dynamo, a highly available Key-Value storage system, that some core services of Amazon uses to provide an "always-on" experience. - To achieve high availability: - Dynamo sacrifices Consistency, under certain failure scenarios. - It makes extensive use of object versioning. - Application-assisted conflict resolution. ## Introduction - 🔙
